Hey there!

The future is now and we are here to prove it.

We’ve created an app for a smart home. With its help, users can control everything around them having only the phone in hands. Smart home management is an innovative way to make digital experience bloom.

Create a unique environment at home by tailoring the lights, sounds, and temperature to your taste and mood.

Be sure your house is protected with the CCTV integration, doors and windows blocking.

Design your own personalized scenarios: make the kettle boil, curtains open and music play at the time when your alarm clock goes off.
Building a connected network of home appliances has never been this easy. We’re so proud of this work!
